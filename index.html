Committees And Agendas

Here are the various committees and agendas



UN Security Council

The United Nations Security Council (UNSC) is one of the six principal organs of the United Nations and has the primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security. Through our agenda for this year we will be analysing the Russian-Ukrainian crisis, understanding more deeply the stance of various nations and the importance of UNSC.





UNCSW

The United Nations Commission on the Status of Women is the principal global intergovernmental body exclusively dedicated to the promotion of gender equality and the empowerment of women. It is instrumental in promoting women‚Äôs rights, documenting the reality of women‚Äôs lives throughout the world and shaping global standards on gender equality. With respect to the recent overturning of Roe vs Wade, we present before you a topic of great relevance ‚ÄúCriminalizing abortion : Pro-Life or ProChoice?‚Äù





AIPPM

The All India Political Parties Meet is a bilingual committee which is a supplementary body to the parliament without any legislative provisions. It is typically called upon before a formal parliamentary session to save time and enhance efficiency & accountability. This year we hope to have a heated discussion on the topic ‚ÄúDiscussing internal threats to Secularism in India with special reference to the issue of Kashmiri Pandits‚Äù





Historical UNGA

By giving an archival aspect to the largest organ of the United Nations, we bring to you our Historical Committee, the (H)UNGA. With the chance of participating in pairs through double delegation, a comprehensive discussion on the topic "Addressing and contemplating the current and imminent threat of a Second World War" shall be held.





UNHRC

The United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC) is a United Nations body whose mission is to promote and protect human rights around the world. As said by Antonio Gutterres, ‚ÄúHuman Rights cannot be confiscated by dictators and erased by poverty‚Äù. Keeping this in mind, our delegates will exhibit their public speaking and diplomacy skills under the topic ‚ÄúPlight of Refugees :- Exploitation and Rehabilitation‚Äù





UNEP

The United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) has been the global authority that sets the environmental agenda, promotes the coherent implementation of the environmental dimension of sustainable development within the United Nations system and serves as an authoritative advocate for the global environment. Through our beginners committee (Only for classes VI-VIII), we hope to be the starting point of the MUN Journeys of various delegates. We welcome them to showcase their debating skills and discuss the topic of ‚ÄúEnvironmental Degradation of Earth : Threats and Reforms‚Äù





Fictional Committee

Avengers are the earth‚Äôs mightiest superheroes. They embark on adventurous journeys to save the world. The fictional committee allows you to relish the happenings in the Multiverse. The responsibility lies with you to save the Multiverse. Through our agenda ‚ÄúMarvel: Multiverse Saga‚Äù we encourage our delegates to put up a fine battle!





Futuristic Committee

The Futuristic Crisis Committee is a distinctive and eccentric simulation of an imaginary timeline having futuristic scope. The committee operates within a given time frame. For the first time in the History of Vezolution, we have a committee having a ‚ÄúClassified Agenda‚Äù, meaning that the agenda is not predecided, making it the most challenging, dynamic and interesting committee.







International Press

Journalists, Caricaturists and Photographers together constitute the International Press. Instead of representing the interests of a single country, the International Press Delegation allows participants to act as unbiased journalists, reporting on the proceedings of each simulation in the Conference.

The world is one family. This one important moral value in the Indian society that drives the students of DPS Faridabad is instrumental in organising the Model United Nations, a simulation of the UN every year.
The world front has always been a conjunction of pleasant and unpleasant events. The scenario is no different today. The United Nations, therefore, plays an important role of addressing such issues, developing friendly relations among Nations, and harmonizing the actions of all.
As a premier educational institution, the school has always been striving to instil in the students the skills of problem solving, conflict resolving, working in harmony etc. VEZOLUTION thus came into existence 7 years back. This educational simulation helps students from various backgrounds and age groups come together and learn about diplomacy, international relations and the UN.
I am happy that the resilient Dipsites continued the tradition of organising the MUN even in the face of unforeseeable challenges.

I look forward to wide participation by young and promising delegates, who will come together to find solutions to global issues, while playing the role of representatives of different countries in a UN like simulation.
Best wishes to all the budding delegates.
